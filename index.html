UFC 315: Belal Muhammad is set to defend his welterweight title against Jack Della Maddalena on May 10, 2025, in Montreal. Della Maddalena steps in as a replacement for the injured Shavkat Rakhmonov, bringing an impressive undefeated streak since 2016 with seven consecutive UFC wins.

Fight Cancellation: The highly anticipated bout between Benoit Saint-Denis and Joel Alvarez has been canceled due to Alvarez suffering a hand injury. Potential replacements are being considered to keep Saint-Denis on the card.
